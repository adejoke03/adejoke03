Easy Rocky Road Recipe

Cook time: 5 minutes

Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 4 digestive biscuits
  • 50g milk chocolate
  • 25g dark chocolate
  • 2 tbsp golden syrup
  • 30g unsalted butter
  • 30g mini marshmallows
  • 20g raisins (optional)
  • 20g chopped nuts (optional)

Ingredients

  1. Crush the biscuits: Break the digestive biscuits into small chunks. Don't crush them too finely; you want some bite-sized pieces.
  2. Melt the chocolate mixture: In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the milk chocolate, dark chocolate, butter, and golden syrup. Microwave in 20-second bursts, stirring each time until smooth.
  3. Combine the ingredients: Stir in the biscuit pieces, mini marshmallows, raisins, and chopped nuts (if using) into the melted chocolate mixture.
  4. Chill: Line a small container or two ramekins with parchment paper, and spoon in the rocky road mixture. Press it down slightly to compact it.
  5. Set and serve: Place in the fridge to set for 30 minutes if you want it firm, or enjoy straight away for a softer, gooey version!
